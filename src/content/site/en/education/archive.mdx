# Archive

The Institute of Heroism and Catastrophe of European Jewry in Jerusalem - Yad Vashem, has long been studying the tragic events of the Nazi occupation. This institute awards the title "Righteous Among the Nations" to individuals who saved Jews from certain death.

You can follow this [link](https://pravedniki.com.ua/) to try to find the Righteous Among the Nations from Ukraine by name or date of birth.
