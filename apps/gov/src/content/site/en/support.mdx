# Help Keep the Memory of Babyn Yar Alive

The National Historical and Memorial Reserve "Babyn Yar" is Ukraine's government
institution dedicated to preserving the memory of Babyn Yar. We research the
history of the site, collect and preserve survivor testimonies and archival
materials, and develop educational programs and exhibitions at the
[Living Memory Exhibition Center](/en/education/living-memory-exhibition-center).

Our mission is to ensure that the history of Babyn Yar remains accessible,
meaningful, and relevant for present and future generations.

Today, as Ukraine once again faces the reality of war, this mission has become
even more urgent. Preserving memory is an essential part of safeguarding our
identity, defending human dignity, protecting historical truth, and strengthening
democratic values.

## Your contribution helps us

- preserve archival collections, historical documents, and personal testimonies;
- expand the Babyn Yar Digital Archive;
- advance historical research;
- develop exhibitions and educational programs;
- care for the memorial landscape and the site of remembrance;
- provide open access to knowledge about Babyn Yar in Ukraine and around the
  world.

## Every Donation Matters

Every contribution, regardless of its size, helps preserve the history of Babyn
Yar and ensures that future generations can continue to learn from it.

## Ways to Support Our Work

### Make a Donation

Support the work of the National Historical and Memorial Preserve "Babyn Yar"
through the NGO Center for Jewish Culture "Living Memory."
[Learn more about the NGO.](/en/support/living-memory-center)

### Become a Corporate Partner

Partner with us to support educational, research, and memorial initiatives that
preserve the history of Babyn Yar and promote public understanding.

### Become a Volunteer

Contribute your time and expertise to educational, cultural, and memorial
projects that help preserve and communicate the history of Babyn Yar.
