The "Living Memory" Exhibition Center, operating on the reserve's grounds and
established during Russia's full-scale invasion of Ukraine in 2022, serves as a
space for dialogue, reflection, and the recognition of the importance of
preserving memory. It hosts exhibitions, discussions, and educational projects
that analyze historical lessons and their relevance to the present day.

Address: 46A Yuriy Illienko Street\
Hours: Daily from 10:00 AM to 7:00 PM\
Admission: Free
