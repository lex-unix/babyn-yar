# NGO Center for Jewish Culture "Living Memory"

To provide a transparent and effective framework for charitable support, the NGO
Center for Jewish Culture "Living Memory" was established to assist the National
Historical and Memorial Preserve "Babyn Yar."

Working in close partnership with the Preserve, the organization directs all
charitable contributions exclusively toward supporting its mission and developing
its educational, research, archival, and memorial projects.

All donations from individuals, businesses, international partners, and
philanthropic organizations are received through the NGO.

## Your Support Helps Us

- preserving the Babyn Yar Archive;
- conducting historical research;
- developing exhibitions;
- creating educational programs.
